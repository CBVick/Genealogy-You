Research Resources

There are many useful resources available for genealogy research. Some are online, while others can be found in libraries, archives, and local historical societies.

Online Databases

  • Ancestry
  • FamilySearch
  • Find a Grave
  • U.S. Census Records

Local Resources

Visit local libraries, courthouses, churches, cemeteries, and historical societies. These places often hold records that are not available online.

Government Records

Government documents such as birth, marriage, death, land, probate, and military records can provide valuable information about your ancestors.

Helpful Research Tip

Always compare information from more than one source. Names, dates, and spellings may vary, so checking multiple records helps improve accuracy.
